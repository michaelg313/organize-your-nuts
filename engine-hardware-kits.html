What's Inside

Every Kit Includes the Essentials

No guessing, no hunting for stragglers. Our kits are built around complete engine reassembly from the bottom end up.

Head Bolts / Head Studs

High-tensile cylinder head fasteners for a proper, leak-free seal. Available in OEM-replacement or performance stud kits.

Main Cap Bolts

Precision main bearing cap fasteners to keep your crankshaft properly clamped under high-load conditions.

Connecting Rod Bolts

Critical bottom-end hardware for securing rod caps. Stretch-type bolts with recommended torque and angle specs included.

Intake Manifold Studs & Bolts

Stainless or black oxide intake hardware in the correct thread pitch for your application, with washers included.

Exhaust Header Bolts

High-temp rated exhaust fasteners with anti-seize coating to survive heat cycling without galling or breaking on removal.

Cam Retaining & Timing Hardware

Camshaft thrust plate bolts, timing cover fasteners, and cam gear bolts -- the small stuff that always goes missing.

Oil Pan & Valley Cover Bolts

Full sets of oil pan rail bolts and valley/valley pan fasteners with replacement crush washers where applicable.

Accessory & Dress Fasteners

Alternator bracket, water pump, and front accessory bolts so your engine bay looks as clean as it runs.
